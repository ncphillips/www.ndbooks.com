With renowned gallery director Christine Burgin, New Directions presents books that are for reading, looking and touching in equal measure. The co-publications present full-color facsimile images of working drafts and new-found masterpieces. They offer never-before-possible glimpses into the process of two literary greats, Emily Dickinson and Robert Walser.
