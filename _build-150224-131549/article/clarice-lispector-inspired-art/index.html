Clarice Lispector Inspired Art!

  • Published

Clarice Lispector

This is too good to keep to ourselves.

Bookseller Jenn Witte, of Skylight Books in Los Angeles, sent along the illustration you see below, saying:

I wanted to send along a drawing I did of the fabulous four Lispectors. They have changed my life in the last month, seriously.

More of Jenn’s book-inspired art can be found on her Tumblr page. And while we’re at it, here are few early reviews of the titles in the Los Angeles Times and The Coffin Factory, along with an excerpt from Near to the Wild Heart in Electric Literature’s "Recommended Reading".
